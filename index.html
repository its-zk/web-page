our journey

  • 2009-2011

    Our Humble Beginnings

    As we were on a goal to achieve all the skills (photography, videography and editing) there was a time that we decided in order to use them properly we shall begin in offering services.

  • March 2011

    An Agency is Born

    After lower level of gigs and jobs our team decided to make it official and forming an agency that can offer all the services mentioned above.

    Transition to Full Service


    So...Once upon a time a photographer needed space fast. One of those last minute shoots. Where could you go to see at a glance; facilities, rates and be able to touch base direct...? There wasn't a site that put shoot space all in one place Until the creation of Photoclock.com.
